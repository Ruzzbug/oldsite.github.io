PROFILE

Completed the Bachelor of Design Computing in 2021 at Sydney University. I have developed skills in Unity, Unreal Engine, UI, UX, graphic design front-end web dev, graphic design, film, photo, object oriented programming in Java, JS and python, media creation and editing including CGI, video and photo. I have professional experience as a 3D generalist working as a subcontractor.

I am a mentor for a FIRST robotics team, a lead mentor at TinkerTank, a STEAM makerspace for school age kids, and I coach a high school basketball team. I also have a keen interest in Virtual Reality and have been building my skills in VR and digital development since high school.

I have received a national FIRST Lego League innovation award and won the NSW award for the best Senior Student Project at the iAwards in 2019, hosted by the Australian Information Industry Association (AIIA).

I have a Working with Children certificate and have completed the Responsible Service of Alcohol and Responsible Conduct of Gambling courses.
